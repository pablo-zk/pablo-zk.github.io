Strategic Plan

To start the project we initially identified and analyzed the project so we could find solutions and select the ones that seemed most viable to us

1

First step

The first step of the CuatroVientos Strategic Plan 18-21 was to analyze the SRSE of the center.

3

Third step

Then, from the Internalization team we are requested and given the opportunity to be the leading Erasmus project on the carbon footprint with the aim of being able to develop the environmental dimension of the Centre.

2

Second step

Later on we do diagnosis using the INNOVARSE methodology.

Project procedure

In order to carry the project forward direction appoints those responsible with the aim of being able to energize the project, this team is composed of the team of axes: internalization, commitment and innovation + an external environmental advisor.

First step

Once these steps have been completed, we analized the situation of the center and the we start making the CHALLENGE of the project "TO BE NEUTRAL CENTER IN EMISSIONS"

Second step

In order to analyze the situation of the center, an environmental awareness day was used so the students could measure the carbon footprint produced by the Center and subsequently several possible actions were proposed in order to reduce and compensate for the emission we produced.

Main activity

One activity, which we will talk about a little more deeply later, was the Gymkana of awareness, made on September 21 to October 16.

Reduction measures

1. The first step is the certification that 100% of the energy consumed at the Centre is renewable energy.

2. The second measure is the installation of a renewable energy production system in the center.

Center activities

1. Art Recyclers (workshop that consisted of making a painting using plugs or material called "garbage")

2. SDG Training

Cultural Week

As for the Cultural Week of the Centre, activities are carried out to achieve the proposed challenge.

Compensation measures

The only compensation measure is to plant 100 trees.
