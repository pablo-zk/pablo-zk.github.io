STOP CO2 - CF TRAIN IN VET

For a long time Cuatrovientos has been interested in improving the environment, so we decided to colaborate in a project called "Stop CO2" which have some other goals apart from improving our home Environment.

Read More

ORIGIN

Cuatrovientos is an Integrated Vocational Training Center, managed by a cooperative. We are looking for be an emission free center, so we are working on an international cooperative project.

As an educational centre, we decided to develop a project that could let us to inculcate such values as cooperative work or the relevance of the environment nowadays.

PROJECT

This project is divided into 3 different parts.

What is it?

CFTrain in VET/STOP CO2 is an international project about sustainability and climate change, based on calculating and reducing the Carbon Footprint in a vocational training center.

About
GOALS
  • Reduce the carbon emissions of the center.
  • Sensitize and raise awareness of society.
  • Educate our students as future professionals in responsible consumption, responsible economy and social responsibility.
  • Combat climate change.
About
PROGRESS

For achieve our goals and above all for reach to be an emission free center, we are doing some different activities, creating strategic plans and colaborating in ACBD methodology sharing our project.
